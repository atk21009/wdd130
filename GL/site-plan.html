Overview

Purpose

Welcome to Gymlist, a site created to help individuals progress or start their healthy lifestyle. This site is geared for any beginner or intermittent or even advanced trainees. Advice, different tricks and basic understanding for training is provided to help you along your way to a healthier lifestyle. Below are also some basic calculators and different tools to help you get started. Some sample workout programs are also provided. We here at GymList want everyone to succeed in a healthier lifestyle. Now let's start the journey for a healthier lifestyle and the best versions we can be.

Audience

People who are wanting to begin, have already started, or who have been working towards a healthier lifestyle

Content

Home page

Home page is designed to get individuals interested in having a more healthier lifestyle. The point of the home page is to try to point the audiance is a good directions in order to get started with their healthier lifestyle. The home page directs the audiance to a clear purpose and benefits of becoming healthier. With an easy structured home page it is designed to give people a brief understanding of why its important to have a healthy lifestyle and how to start having a healthier lifestyle.

Images for the Home page

[Page 2]

Page 2 is designed for having a little more context in how to achieve a healthier lifestlye and what others do in order to achieve it. It provides a good base block for others to get an understanding of how it can be easily achieved. It provides the audiance with confirmation that they can achieve a healthy lifestlye without taking a life time to achieve. It shows the process of losing or gaining weight as well as how to build muscle if the reader is interested in it.

Images for the Page 2

[Page 3]

Page 3 is designed to give the reader tool/tools to help them achieve their goals. Provided on page 3 will be a calorie calculator which is very helpful to help gain or lose weight. It provides others with an understanding of how much they should be eating a day, and what is best for them. It will have a couple inputs like height, weight, etc. and will spit out their calorie goal for each day. Providing the reader with a base platform of how much they should eat in a day.
